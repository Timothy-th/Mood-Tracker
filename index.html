The Heart Behind Mood Tracking

Our moods guide us—they show when we need rest, joy, or care. In the
rush of life, it’s easy to forget to check in with ourselves. Mood
tracking helps us notice not just the hard times, but the small wins and
quiet moments too. Your feelings matter.

What is a mood tracker?

A mood tracker is a tool that helps users keep track of how they feel each day. By recording their emotions, users can better understand their mental and emotional patterns over time. This project lets users select their mood, chat with a virtual assistant, and see a custom avatar that reflects their current feeling.

Why we should use a mood tracker?

Using a mood tracker helps you become more aware of your emotions and patterns over time. It can improve self-understanding, support mental well-being, and make it easier to spot triggers or trends. By reflecting on how you feel, you can make better decisions, build emotional resilience, and take care of your mental health.

Advantages of Using a Mood Tracker

Using a mood tracker helps you stay in tune with your emotions and notice patterns over time. It supports mental well-being and helps you identify triggers or emotional shifts. By reflecting on how you feel each day, you can build resilience, make healthier choices, and take better care of your mental health.
